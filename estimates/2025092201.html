Scope of Work — Tall Fescue Reseeding

Reseed the lawn with high-quality seed and set it up to thrive. Below is everything included so you know exactly what to expect.

Site prep
  • Mow existing turf to 2"–2.5" (as requested) and remove clippings from seeded areas.
  • Dethatching where thatch exceeds 1/4" (spot-based).
  • Core Aeration for optimal root oxygenation.
  • Light raking to expose soil contact for seed.
Seeding & finish
  • Seed: 50lbs recommended for lot size 4,600 sq ft.
  • Top-dress: Light compost or planting mix to improve seed-to-soil contact (approximately 1/8\" layer — ~48 cu ft ≈ 1.8 cu yd for 4,600 sq ft).
  • Starter fertilizer: Slow-release starter to support root development.
  • Seed spreading with rake-in for optimal coverage and contact.
Watering & establishment
  • Initial watering plan: Light, frequent watering (2–3 times daily for first 10–14 days) to keep the seedbed moist but not flooded.
  • After germination (7–21 days) reduce frequency, increase depth — transition to 2–3 deep waterings per week over 4–6 weeks.
  • Mowing: First mow when seedlings reach ~3.5"; keep mower height 2.5" for first few mowings.
  • Follow-up visit: One complimentary inspection 14–21 days after seeding to check germination and offer recommendations.
