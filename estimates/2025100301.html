Scope of Work
  • Site prep: low mow & clean, remove debris.
  • Powered dethatching (aggressive, multiple passes as needed).
  • Core aeration—deep & overlapping passes to relieve compaction.
  • Topdressing: heavy organic amendment (screened topsoil + finished compost + aged manure) — delivered and spread.
  • Seeding: premium Sun & Shade tall fescue blend (increased seeding rate for difficult establishment).
  • Starter fertilizer: 10‑20‑10 applied at increased rate for establishment.
  • Clean-up, client briefing, and watering/mowing instructions; follow‑up checks at 14 and 30 days.
Important: This scope is a full renovation. Light overseed methods will not give a reliable lawn on this site.

Why the heavy amendment?
Construction‑grade clay is compacted, low in organic matter, and hydrophobic in spots. Heavy topdressing rebuilds pore space, improves water infiltration, and gives seeded turf a medium to establish.

Establishment & Watering (brief)

Newly renovated soil and seed require reliable moisture. The schedule below is a condensed summary — provide to client as handout on completion.

  • Days 0–14: Keep surface consistently moist. Run short irrigation cycles 2–3× per day early morning and late morning; avoid evening irrigation.
  • Days 15–42: Transition to deeper, less frequent waterings (0.5–0.75" per session) 2–3× per week depending on weather.
  • After 6–8 weeks: Maintain 1.0–1.5" per week depending on sun exposure and soil moisture.
Technician will provide a printed watering chart customized to this property on completion.
