Front Garden Bed Renewal

Refined, Durable, and Designed for Long-Term Success

Project Overview

This project focuses on strengthening and refining the existing garden bed using plant selections that have already proven successful in this environment.

The updated design emphasizes fuller planting, simplified plant selection, and improved layout to create a more cohesive, polished, and low-maintenance landscape.

Design Goals

  • Increase planting density for a more finished, high-end look
  • Use proven plants that thrive in part sun and tree-shaded conditions
  • Reduce long-term maintenance through groundcover expansion
  • Create layered structure with year-round visual appeal
  • Add seasonal color for immediate impact

Scope of Work

  • Plant layout and professional spacing
  • Installation of all shrubs, perennials, and groundcover
  • Integration with existing successful plants
  • Mulch application for moisture retention and clean appearance
  • Final cleanup and bed detailing

Design Improvements

  • Simplified plant palette focused on proven performers
  • Stronger grouping and repetition for a cohesive look
  • Increased groundcover to eliminate bare areas
  • Improved balance between structure and seasonal color
