Front Garden Bed Renewal

Refined, Durable, and Designed for Long-Term Success

Project Overview

This project strengthens and refines the existing garden bed using plant material that performs well in this specific environment.

The design focuses on density, repetition, and layered structure to create a cohesive, established landscape with reduced long-term maintenance.
