Lucinda's Garden Bed Renewal

East-Facing Under-Maple Installation | Layered Shade-Tolerant Design

Design Intent

This installation rebuilds a previously underperforming bed into a structured, shade-adapted planting system designed for Edmond’s heat, filtered light, and irrigation support under mature canopy conditions.

Planting Plan

Columnar Blue Atlas Cedar (2)

Structural blue-toned evergreen anchors flanking the front window for year-round form.

Indigo Flair Mahonia (5)

Shade-adapted evergreen massing for texture and stability.

Black Barlow Columbine (6)

Early-season bloom layer supporting hummingbirds in filtered shade.

Woodland Phlox (12)

Spring groundcover carpet for soil stabilization and bloom density.

Endless Summer Hydrangea (3)

Primary summer focal shrubs with repeat bloom performance.

American Beautyberry (2)

Mosquito repelling Oklahoma native edible flowering perennial.

Purple Wild Bergamot (5)

Pollinator-supporting mid-layer perennial with aromatic foliage.

Orange Daylily (3)

Reliable seasonal color with strong drought tolerance.

Lavender (5)

Fragrant edge planting supporting pollinators and ambiance.

Purple Balloon Flower (7)

Long-lasting mid-summer bloom accent layer.

Salvia greggii (7)

Heat-tolerant flowering structure for extended bloom season.

Impatiens (36)

Seasonal color massing for shaded irrigated areas.

Lantana (12)

Sun-edge annual color with pollinator support.
