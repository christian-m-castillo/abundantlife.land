Front Garden Bed Restoration Proposal

Client: Steven Mullins
Prepared by: Christian Castillo
Project Area: Approx. 120–150 sq ft front landscape bed
Total: $495

Project Goal

Restore the front garden bed to a clean, defined, HOA-compliant condition by removing the existing ornamental tree, clearing unwanted growth, correcting mulch overspill, and improving overall bed presentation.

Work to Be Completed

Remove Small Tree Remove the existing stressed ornamental tree from the front bed.
Clear Sprouts & Weeds Remove stress shoots, weeds, and unwanted volunteer growth throughout the bed.
Cut Stump Low Cut stump flush to grade or as close to grade as practical.
Restore Bed Edge Clean up and redefine the visible perimeter of the landscape bed.
Correct Mulch Overspill Pull displaced mulch back into the bed and clean up overflow from hard surfaces.
Improve Bed Presentation Create a cleaner, more open appearance for HOA review and curb appeal.
Haul Away Debris Remove tree debris, weeds, sprouts, and cleanup waste from the property.
Final Cleanup Leave the front bed neat, open, and ready for future planting.
Completed Result: Tree Removed • Bed Cleaned • Edge Defined • HOA-Compliant
