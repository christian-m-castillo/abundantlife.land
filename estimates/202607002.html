Landscape Stewardship Program
Client: Steven Mullins
Property: 3204 Cotswold Square, Norman, OK
Visit Frequency: Monthly
Billing: Monthly

Program Goal

Provide ongoing professional care for the property's ornamental landscape, helping maintain an attractive, healthy, and HOA-compliant appearance throughout the year.
Landscape Stewardship Membership
$185 / Month
Monthly Visit Includes:
  • Garden bed inspection and care
  • Hand weeding as needed
  • Shrub shaping and light pruning
  • Deadheading and seasonal grooming
  • Mulch redistribution and touch-up maintenance
  • Monitoring plant health concerns
  • Property presentation review
  • HOA appearance maintenance
Winter Services Include:
  • Snow and ice clearing of primary walkway as needed
  • Seasonal ornamental pruning
  • Winter garden cleanup
  • Monitoring trees and shrubs for storm damage
Expected Result:
A consistently maintained landscape without the need to schedule individual cleanup visits throughout the year.
Included
  • One scheduled stewardship visit per month
  • Routine horticultural maintenance
  • Light seasonal pruning
  • Landscape observations and recommendations
  • Small snow and ice events affecting the primary walkway
Not Included
  • New plant installation
  • Mulch replacement
  • Tree removals
  • Major pruning projects
  • Storm damage cleanup
  • Landscape renovations
  • Irrigation repairs
  • Materials and plant purchases
