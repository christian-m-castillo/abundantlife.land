Abundant Life Land and Tree LLC:
Monthly Stewardship Service Agreement

This agreement is made between Abundant Life Land and Tree LLC ("Service Provider") and the undersigned client ("Client") for monthly horticultural and land stewardship services.

Client Information

1. Service Package

Flat hourly rate of $57/hour. Services will be tailored monthly based on seasonal needs and client goals.

2. Scope of Work (as applicable)

  • Garden design & horticultural consultation
  • Landscape feature installation
  • Pruning, weeding, seasonal maintenance
  • Soil building: compost, inoculants, pH balancing
  • Weekly electric mowing (included in top 3 tiers)
  • Invasive species control, beneficial species planting
  • Inspection, selection, sourcing and pickup of materials
  • Management for fruit/floral yield, ecosystem health, human & pet health

3. Billing & Payment

  • Invoices issued at the start of each month
  • Payment due within 7 days
  • Additional hours billed at $57/hour, with prior approval
  • Late payments may delay service
  • Cost of materials will be invoiced separately as needed prior to pickup

4. Scheduling & Communication

  • Scheduled on mutually agreed dates
  • Weather or seasonal delays will be communicated
  • Open communication encouraged

5. Termination & Adjustments

  • 14-day written notice required to cancel or change tiers
  • Hours do not roll over, but flexibility may be offered

6. Liability & Safety

  • Abundant Life Land and Tree LLC is fully insured to $2M for landscaping and tree services
  • All work performed with ecological and site safety in mind
  • Client should disclose hazards, boundaries, and sensitive areas

7. Agreement

By signing below, the Client agrees to the terms of this monthly service agreement and authorizes Abundant Life Land and Tree LLC to begin care of their property as outlined above.

Thank you for allowing Abundant Life Land and Tree LLC to care for your land. We look forward to restoring beauty, health, and abundance to your outdoor space.
