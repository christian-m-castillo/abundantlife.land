Our Household Operating Page

Mission: Our family exists to serve God, steward land and people, care faithfully for those entrusted to us, and live as a reliable presence in our community.

How to Use This Page

This page exists to help us stay oriented, calm, and united. It is not a scorecard or a tool for control. We return here when things feel confusing, tense, or uncertain, so that decisions can be grounded in shared commitments rather than pressure or emotion.

This is a living document. We revise it together as our family grows.

Guiding Principles

Service to God precedes comfort.
Stewardship bests consumption.
Caretaking is a daily practice, not a mood.
Reliability matters more than intensity.
Decisions should support long-term rootedness.
Meet each other and ourselves with Grace.

Leadership & Decision Framework

Joint Decisions
  • Major financial commitments
  • Land or housing decisions
  • Education and childcare choices
  • Schedule changes affecting the whole family
Individual Authority
  • Day-to-day task execution
  • Pre-agreed domains of responsibility
  • Routine purchases within budget
High-Stakes Safety Rule

In safety-critical moments, decisive action takes priority. Emotional processing and discussion happen afterward, without blame.
