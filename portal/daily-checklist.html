Daily Pre-Work Checklist

1. Personal Readiness

  • Rested & alert
  • Hydrated & fed
  • Mentally prepared

2. PPE Check

  • Helmet + face shield/goggles
  • Hearing protection
  • Gloves
  • Chainsaw chaps / pants
  • Safety Footwear

3. Tools & Equipment

  • Chainsaw: sharpen, oil/fuel, batteries
  • Hand tools: sharpen, function check, stage
  • Harness / climb lines: inspected, hitches secured
  • Ladders / rigging lines: safely staged & ready

4. Site Assessment

  • Overhead hazard identification
  • Terrain safety evaluation
  • Debris / trip hazard removal
  • Weather condition evaluation
  • Valuable obstacle declaration
  • Drop zones clear & marked

5. Communication & Roles

  • Morning briefing completed
  • Roles & tasks confirmed
  • Call-and-response / hand signals reviewed
  • Emergency contacts & assembly points known

6. Stop-Work Authority

  • Everyone reminded: stop work if unsafe
  • Hazards discussed & acknowledged
  • Crew readiness confirmed

7. Prayer and Work Start

  • Prayer: Gratitude, Safety & Discernment
  • Crew in positions, final assessment
  • Commence operations
