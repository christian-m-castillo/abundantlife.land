Scope of Work

Abundant Life Land and Tree LLC agrees to furnish labor, plant material, mulch, soil amendments, and associated installation services necessary to complete the Lucinda Garden Bed Renewal project as described in the accompanying proposal found at https://abundantlife.land/estimates/20260511001.html

Work includes installation of:

  • Structural evergreen shrubs and ornamental focal plants
  • Shade-tolerant flowering perennials and seasonal color
  • Groundcover and pollinator-supporting plant material
  • Mulch and soil preparation as needed for installation
  • General bed shaping and planting layout execution

Minor field adjustments may be made during installation to account for root conditions, irrigation layout, drainage, plant availability, or overall visual balance.

While installation is performed using professional horticultural practices appropriate for Edmond, Oklahoma conditions, Abundant Life Land and Tree LLC provides no express or implied warranty regarding long-term plant survival, bloom performance, growth rate, or appearance after installation.

Long-term performance depends heavily upon watering consistency, environmental conditions, and ongoing maintenance performed after project completion.
